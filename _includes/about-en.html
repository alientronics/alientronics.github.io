About Alientronics

The Alientronics is a company of Porto Alegre - RS focused on developing Internet solutions of Things, aiming to monitor and better manage any device that you can connect to the internet.

TPMS Sensor is the first product being developed by the company in partnership with the CETEMP / SENAI. Intelligent Monitoring System project for tires (TPMS) was one of the Santander Entrepreneurship Award 2015 winners.
