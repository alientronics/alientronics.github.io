About Alientronics

Alientronics is a company of Porto Alegre - RS focused on developing Internet solutions of Things, while aiming to monitor and better manage any device with which you can connect to the internet.

The TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System) Sensor is the first product being developed by Alientronics in partnership with ISI-SIM / SENAI and Avansat. The intelligent Monitoring System project created for tires (TPMS) was one of the Santander Entrepreneurship Award 2015 winners and Modern Transport & Innovation in Transportation TruckPad Award 2016.
